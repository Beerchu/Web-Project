About Us

At Five Stars Cinema, we believe booking a movie ticket should be as simple as watching the movie itself. Designed with speed, clarity, and accessibility in mind, our “select–seat–pay” flow removes all unnecessary steps.

We aim to deliver a smooth, one-minute reservation experience — fair prices, instant e-tickets, and a touch of cinematic elegance.

Founded by cinema lovers, Five Stars Cinema is more than just a booking platform — it's a celebration of the big screen. We aim to bring people together through stories,emotions, and unforgettable movie nights.

From the moment you choose your seat to the second the lights dim, we're with you every step of the way.

Sık Sorulanlar

How do I receive my e-ticket after booking?

Once your payment is confirmed, your e-ticket and QR code will be sent via email and displayed on the “My Tickets” page.

Which payment methods do you support?

We accept credit/debit cards and selected e-wallet solutions. Payments are secured with 3D Secure protection.

How long are my seats reserved?

Once selected, your seats are held for 10 minutes before the reservation times out automatically.

Can I cancel my ticket?

Cancellations or changes can be made up to 2 hours before the movie start time, depending on the theatre’s policy.

How are age restrictions applied?

Age ratings such as +13, +16 are displayed on each movie page. Tickets cannot be purchased for restricted showings.
